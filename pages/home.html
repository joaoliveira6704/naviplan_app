NaviPlan is an innovative and user-friendly app designed to simplify flight planning for pilots and aviation professionals. Whether you're preparing for a commercial flight or a personal adventure, NaviPlan provides you with the tools you need to ensure a safe and efficient journey.

Key Features

  • Accurate Performance Calculations: Calculate essential performance metrics, including fuel consumption, range, climb rates, and takeoff/landing distances for various aircraft types.
  • Document Indexing: Easily organize and access important flight documents such as operational manuals, checklists, and charts all in one place.
  • Customizable Settings: Tailor calculations to your specific aircraft and flight conditions to ensure the most accurate results.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate the app effortlessly with an intuitive design that caters to both professionals and aviation enthusiasts.

Why Choose NaviPlan?

NaviPlan is completely free and open-source, making it accessible to everyone in the aviation community. With our commitment to accuracy and ease of use, we empower pilots to make informed decisions and enhance their flight planning experience.
